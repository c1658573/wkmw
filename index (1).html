What's is in the plan?
The student will get two levels of coding tasks. one level suitable for first year and the second level is suitable for second year or third year. Students of third year will be able to come and work on their final project. In each level, first solve the first challenge and than continue to the following task and we will see who is the one that will go far than everyone. Every student will go forward in his programming's skills, for sure. Also, we are sure that these are the first steps of a long way and a career that will keep developing.
